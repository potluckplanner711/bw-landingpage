Potluck Planner

Let us take the stress out of your next potluck

This app allows people to see the date on their calender, signup with what dish they are going to bring so everything that is needed is filled, and even to confirm when someone is going to attend. It also give the user the ability to invite people, and even suggest the dishes that people might want to bring incase they are unsure.
